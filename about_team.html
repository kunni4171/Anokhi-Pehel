    Welcome to Anokhi पहल

    Anokhi पहल is an initiative taken by the students of MNNIT to help the children of the economically weaker sections of the society by providing them an education in the best possible way.

    Contact Us

    How we started how it's going

    Started with a few teachers and just 20 students, today we have a team of about 60 teachers shaping the life of more than 250 students. Our students have shown great performance in various fields.

    Contact Us

    How we Approach

    Under this initiative, We teach students from preparatory classes to 12th level teaching them various subjects free of cost. Not only do we develop academic skills of the students but also other extra curricular activities such as painting, dancing, sketching, etc.
